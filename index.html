About Me

Hello! My name is Brent Keath. I hold a bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and am currently studying my M.B.A. in IT Management. I am an app developer, website designer, and game inventor. I also ran a business in college where I developed mobile apps. I live by the golden rule: “Treat others as you wish to be treated.” I have not only managed but been part of numerous teams throughout my life. My goal professionally is to be part of a company where I can help the community around me while personally improving my own skills and well-being. I also love to have fun and make people laugh.
